About Me

I am currently pursuing my bachelor’s degree in computer science at School of Computer and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. Additionally, I am a guest student at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the mentorship of Prof. Lin Gao.

In 2024, I will be joining the Camera Intelligence Lab led by Prof. Boxin Shi at the School of Computer Science, Peking University as a PhD student.

My research interests encompass computer graphics, computer vision, and machine learning, with a particular focus on 3D vision and generative models.
